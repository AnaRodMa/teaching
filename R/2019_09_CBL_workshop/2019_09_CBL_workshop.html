Explore the data

  • Young and healthy adults watched pictures, that were either Negative or Neutral (the emotion_condition variable)
  • For each picture (48 different pictures), they had to rate the Intensity (arousal) and Valence (positive - negative) of the emotion that they felt, as well as the Autobiographical link (how do they personally relate to the content of the picture)
  • 30 min after the presentation, participants had to recall the images
